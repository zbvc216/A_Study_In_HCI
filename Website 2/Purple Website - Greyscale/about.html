About Purple

Our staff team is varied and diverse with individual skills that are applied to every task they carry out. To compliment these skills all staff are given training specific to the services that we offer and these are maintained to meet changing standards.

All staff hold a current DBS (formerly CRB).

Since begining in 2001 We have grown and become the standard for the industry within the 3 counties area.
