Services: We currently provide

  1. Home
  2. Services

Contact

Supported, Supervised, Assessed, or Handover. Whatever your current situation we will work to provide contact to the best of our ability.

Family Assessment

Our family assessments are bespoke to suit a variety of requirements i.e. a couple, single parents or other family members this can be undertaken in our centre in the community or a combination of both.

Domiciliary Care

Assistance with personal care needs. If appropriate, we can offer an holistic package of personal care and activities.

Respite

We offer to support families where an additional hand is needed to enable the children to experience different activities, attends clubs, build social skills, independent skills and other life skills as required.

Preperation Work

We offer preparatory work with children who may not have seen a parent for some time. The work is child led, dependent on their age.

Life Story Work

We can work with children, young people and parents to become familiar and understand their past to help them move forward in a positive way again this is a bespoke package to suit individuals.

Room Hire

We can provide rooms for meetings, local authority or CAFCASS to supervise contacts.

Family Support

Home support to assist families with budgeting, parenting, routines, standards etc. Plan and structure Independent Living Skills for young people leaving the care system and young people with Learning Difficulties.

Out of Hours

Emergency Call-out to support placements in crisis, to support families when Child Protection issues are being identified, Welfare checks, Education Support.
